Common Crop Diseases

At CropGuard, we are vigilant in protecting our crops from various diseases. Below are some common diseases that affect our crops and the preventive measures we take to combat them:

Rust

Rust is a common fungal disease affecting wheat. We prevent it by using resistant varieties and applying organic fungicides at the right time.

Blast

Rice blast is a severe threat to rice crops. We manage it through water management techniques and resistant varieties to keep our crops healthy.

Leaf Blight

Corn leaf blight is controlled by crop rotation and the use of bio-insecticides, ensuring the continued health of our corn crops.
